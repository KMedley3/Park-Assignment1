Welcome To Medley Park!
Located in the beautiful city of Charlotte, NC. We are free to the public and open year round. Please have a look at all our activities that change depending on the season. We have free wifi and also a dog park.

Location

If you're having any trouble finding Medley park check out the map! We're located right off exit 19. Once you pass the QT gas station drive east for 2 miles and the sign to enter the park will be of the left.
